# Keyword Research Report Example

This document provides a template for keyword research deliverables targeting small business owners and teams for a project management software company. The report includes an executive summary with metrics like 150+ keywords analyzed, 23 high-priority opportunities, and 45,000 monthly traffic potential. Top keyword opportunities are organized by priority level—Quick Wins (immediate), Growth Keywords (3-6 months), and GEO Opportunities with AI-citation potential. The content also covers topic clusters with pillar and cluster article recommendations, plus a content calendar with suggested topics, target keywords, and content types for each week. The next steps section outlines priorities for creating landing pages, building pillar content, and implementing internal linking strategies.
