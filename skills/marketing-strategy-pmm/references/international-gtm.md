## US Market Playbook

### Timeline: Months 1-6

| Phase | Activities | Timeline |
|-------|------------|----------|
| Foundation | Set up US legal entity, bank account, payment processing | Month 1 |

| Marketing | Launch US-focused website, LinkedIn ads, content | Months 2-4 |
| Sales | Outbound motion, partner referrals, events | Months 3-6 |

### Channel Mix (US)

| Channel | Budget % | Expected CPL |

| LinkedIn Ads | 30% | $150-200 |
| Content/SEO | 20% | $80-100 |
| Outbound | 25% | $250-300 |
| Events | 15% | $300-400 |
| Partner | 10% | $100-150 |
